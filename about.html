OUR JOURNEY

Our Story

Alpha Computer LTD was founded with a vision to transform the technology landscape in Rwanda. From humble beginnings, we have grown to become one of the most trusted technology solutions providers in the country.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships with global technology leaders, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We have successfully delivered over 500 projects across various sectors including education, corporate, government, and events.

Today, we stand as a testament to what dedication, expertise, and customer focus can achieve. Our team of skilled professionals continues to push boundaries, bringing cutting-edge technology solutions to businesses and institutions throughout Rwanda and beyond.
